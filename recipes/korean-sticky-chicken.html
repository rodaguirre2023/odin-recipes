Korean Sticky Chicken

Description

This Sticky Korean Chicken Recipe is packed with heat, nuttiness, and sweetness. Honey and gochugaru create a peppery glaze that makes this Korean chicken recipe, out of this world. Ready in 20 minutes from start to finish.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Gochugaru
  • Oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Rice vinegar
  • Honey
  • Green onion

Steps

  1. Just lay your boneless chicken pieces on a piece of parchment paper on a solid surface.
  2. Sprinkle gochugaru (Korean red chili flakes) on top of the breasts and place a piece of parchment paper on top.
  3. Pound a few times with a rolling pin or meat tenderizer. This will lock the spices on your chicken giving it a serious boost of flavor.
  4. Pan fry the chicken in a skillet over medium to medium-high heat for a few minutes until itâ€™s fully cooked and transfer the pieces to a plate.
  5. Mix remaining ingredients for the sauce in a bowl and add it to the skillet. Stir and keep cooking until the sauce bubbles, about 1 to 2 minutes.
  6. Return the chicken to the skillet and quickly toss it with the sauce to coat the pieces evenly.
